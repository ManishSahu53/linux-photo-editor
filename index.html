Linux Preview Help

Welcome to this simulated Linux Photo Editor. You can perform standard vector markup operations on any image:

  • Open Image: Click "File" -> "Open" or drag and drop an image into the editor.
  • Select / Move / Resize: In Selection tool mode, click on any shape to select it. Drag it to move, or drag its handles to resize.
  • Add Text: Select the Text tool, click on the canvas, type inside the box, and click outside to save. Double click text to edit.
  • Delete: Select a shape and press Backspace or Delete key.
  • Zoom & Pan: Zoom using mouse wheel or toolbar buttons. Hold Spacebar and drag to pan when zoomed in.
  • Save: Click File -> Export to download your image with all annotations merged in full resolution!
